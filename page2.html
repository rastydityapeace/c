About Me.

A Tomboy in The Body of a Girl Made His Presence in This World On 29 November 2006. Her Father Sagar Bhande and Mother Sneha Bhande Never Stopped Her By Doing The Works of His Likings. She Received More Than 20 Trophies By Performing At Various Dance Shows Like Mumbai Dance Championship , Mega Malhar in 2015 Etc. But The Biggest Achievement of My Life is the Dance Reality Show Super Dancer Season 1. I Was Crowned As The Winner of Super Dancer Having Beaten Masoom, Laxman, Yogesh and Deepali. This Victory is The Combined Efforts of My Choreographer Ruel Dausan and Prashant Dalvi Sir Who Worked So Hard For Making Me India's First Dance Ka Kal. I Was Then Awarded With 23rd SOL Lion Awards in 2017 For Best Dancer. My Filmy Carrier Started With A Tamil Film "Lakshmi" in Which I Got A Chance To Work With Indian Macheal Jackson, Prabhu Deva Sir. After This Movie i Was Awarded With Several More Awards Like Jfw Awards, Anand Viketan Awards, Gallatta Awards Etc. For Best Dancer, Best Child Debutant Actor Etc. I Had Also a Younger Sister Hruday. I Love To Play Banjo. I Was Also Called With The Nickname "Khajoor" and "Red Bull".
